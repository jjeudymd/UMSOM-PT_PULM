Case 2

Pain S/P Fall

Later the same evening, a 39 year old female who is a transfer from an outside hospital s/p fall onto a railroad track. The patient reports that she leaned forward to look for the train and subsequently fell onto the track. She was helped by bystanders and taken to a hospital.

The patient has a past medical history of bipolar disorder and PTSD. You send the patient for imaging and review the imaging with the radiologist.

PHYSICAL EXAMINATION:

  • The patient denies nausea, vomiting, and chest pain.
  • She reports shortness of breath and right upper quadrant abdominal pain.
