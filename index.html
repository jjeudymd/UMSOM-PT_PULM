Pulmonary Small Group 24

Imaging of Pulmonary Disease

LEARNING OBJECTIVES

After completing this small group module, student will be able to:

  • Become familiar with common pathologic processes of the lung and associated imaging

INSTRUCTIONS

  • The pulmonary small group session is comprised of 5 individual case vignettes
  • With each vignette, a series of 3 questions are provided to be reviewed in preparation for the group session and discussion
  • This website is provided as a supplemental resource with a full dataset of images available for each clinical vignette.
  • The provided image viewer is a full-featured tool to evaluate generic radiological image (DICOM format)
